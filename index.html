MEET JULIE

Julie Rowland

President of Quadrivium Advisors, Teacher, Educator, Coach, and Guide

I have always found happiness in the achievements of those I serve. Whether it was guiding my high school students to success as a math teacher or helping my clients reach financial milestones in my financial advisory business, I’ve always been driven to support others in reaching their goals.

For the last 10+ years, I’ve been channeling that passion into helping entrepreneurs lead their businesses with confidence and clarity. Through Peer Advisory Councils and Quadrivium Connections groups, I provide small business owners with a structured support system to navigate the ups and downs of entrepreneurship. These groups allow them to share experiences, gain insights, and find real, objective perspectives from like-minded professionals.

As an entrepreneur myself, I know firsthand the demands of building a business from the ground up. Small business owners often feel isolated and overextended, with little time to step back and objectively assess their operations. That’s where the strength of a peer group can make all the difference. By connecting with others in a council, entrepreneurs benefit from collective wisdom and the accountability needed to reach their goals.

Finding a need for better networking, I formed Quadrivium Connections which offers a unique platform for entrepreneurs and business leaders to engage in structured networking providing invaluable insights, accountability, and support from like-minded professionals. Through these focused connections, members gain fresh perspectives, solve challenges collaboratively, and make smarter, more confident decisions to drive their businesses forward.

I can honestly say that I love what I do. The people I’ve met, the businesses I’ve helped, and the relationships I’ve built have all enriched my life in ways I never expected. Helping others succeed has not only been rewarding professionally but has also added so much meaning and fulfillment to my own journey.
